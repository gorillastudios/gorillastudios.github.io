This weekend I headed down to Cedar Point IL and had a blast photographing the Bigfoot Bandcamp music festival. If you are looking for a truly unique, laid back and intimate atmosphere, this was the place to be.

Jaik Wilis

I wasn't able to get down to the show until early afternoon, but when I arrived Jaik Wilis was taking the stage. I was floored when he opened with a solo acoustic cover of Master of Puppets, beatboxing the rhythm section. Great set and a very unique talent.

Check out the photos from his set here, and you can find more information about Jaik on ###HIS WEBSITE ###.

Evacuate The Earth

Up next was Evacuate The Earth, a three piece band from SOMEWHERE. I love bands that shake things up, and I knew I was in for something special when I the lead singer with a saxaphone, a flute, a bull horn, and her own drum. The bassist had his own theremin which needless to say you don't see very often.

You can see all my photos from there set here and more information about them on ### THEIR WEBSITE ###.

The Voodoo Fix

After a short break The Voodoo Fix stormed onto the stage. They recently moved from LA to Nashville where, from what I saw, they are going to make a huge splash in the local scene and well beyond.

You can see all my photos from there set here and more information about them on ### THEIR WEBSITE ###.

The Krank Daddies

The Krank Daddies are a band that have been rocking for quite some time. Something nice about them here.
