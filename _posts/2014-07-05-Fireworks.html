I already wrote a long post about my 4th of July weekend, so I want to keep this one brief. I selected another shot from our family fireworks display in Indiana as my photo of the week. This is very close to what I had in mind when I setup my tripod - I wanted a wide shot with the fireworks going off from in back of the house with a few people in frame if possible. On my full-frame 5D MkIII at 19mm I was just shy of capturing the entire ark, but I love the overall exposure. Great evening with the family.
