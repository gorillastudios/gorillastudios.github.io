--- layout: post title: NMOS14 - Chicago date: 2014-08-15 ---

I have been extremely upset watching the events unfold in Ferguson MO, so when I saw that they were holding a rally a few blocks from my office I wanted a chance to photograph the event. After work I headed up to Daley Plaza in the heart of the loop where demonstrators started to gather an hour before the event. It was an extremely emotional demonstration with a moment of silence at 6:20pm CST as an act of solidarity with the people of Ferguson. Here are some of my photographs presented without comment.
