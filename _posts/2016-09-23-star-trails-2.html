A few weeks ago I went out and did a star-trail test run where I learned quite a bit about the process and after all was said and done was ready to go out and do it for real on a clear night without the full moon. Of course my excitement was dampened quite a bit when I saw nothing but rain and clouds on the ten day weather report.

As you can see the clouds came and covered the entire sky, the flickering on the ground is from when they sporadically covered the moon. I learned a lot and will be much more confident when I go out during the next new moon to capture something really special. Stay tuned and happy star-gazing!
