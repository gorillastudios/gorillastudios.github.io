A selection of my work will be on display at the Batavia Fine Arts Centre from November to early January. The Fine Arts Centre is part of the Batavia High School at 1399 W Wilson Street, Batavia IL. An evening reception is in the works for mid-December, and I will make an announcement when the details are settled.

Framed prints are available for sale, and unframed matted prints will be available at the reception.
